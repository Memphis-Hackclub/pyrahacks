What Is PyraHacks?

Pyrahacks is an online hackathon for all ages. Hackathons are a great way to test and refine your skills, work on a project with a team of awesome people, and maybe learn something new along the way. Pyrahacks will also serve as a fundraiser for St. Jude Childrenâ€™s Hospital and support their cancer research. This hackathon will also include a number of guest speakers who will lead panels throughout the event. Participation is completely free, but we also encourage you to donate to our St. Jude fundraiser.


Join PyraHacks

Any student is welcome to join PyraHacks for free! Partipants will need to be on a team with 1-3 other people, but we do have a team building Discord channel, so we highly encourage people who don't have a team to join. Join PyraHacks. Signup on Devpost and join the PryaHacks Discord!
